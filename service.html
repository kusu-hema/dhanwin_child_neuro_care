Our Services

Stroke

At Dhanwin Child Neuro Care, we provide comprehensive care and rehabilitation for pediatric stroke patients. Our specialized team utilizes advanced imaging techniques, such as MRI and CT scans, along with cutting-edge treatment modalities to promptly diagnose and manage stroke in children. We understand the critical importance of early intervention, aiming to minimize long-term neurological deficits. Our multidisciplinary approach involves neurologists, physiotherapists, occupational therapists, and speech therapists working collaboratively to develop personalized rehabilitation programs. These programs are tailored to each child's unique needs, ensuring optimal recovery and enhancing their quality of life. We also provide support and education to families, empowering them with the knowledge and skills needed to assist in their child's recovery process.

Neuropathy and Myopathy

Our clinic specializes in the diagnosis and management of nerve and muscle disorders affecting children. We employ state-of-the-art diagnostic tools, including electromyography (EMG) and nerve conduction studies (NCS), to accurately identify conditions such as neuropathies and myopathies. Our comprehensive treatment plans are individualized, incorporating a combination of physical therapy, medication, and lifestyle modifications to improve symptoms and enhance muscle function. We focus on early intervention to prevent disease progression and employ a multidisciplinary approach, involving pediatric neurologists, physiotherapists, and dietitians, to provide holistic care. Our goal is to improve the overall well-being and functional abilities of children with these conditions, enabling them to lead active and fulfilling lives.

Epilepsy and Seizure Disorders

Dhanwin Child Neuro Care offers specialized care for children with epilepsy and seizure disorders. Our comprehensive approach includes advanced diagnostic evaluations such as video EEG monitoring, MRI, and genetic testing to accurately diagnose the type and cause of epilepsy. We provide personalized medication management, optimizing anti-seizure medications to achieve the best possible control with minimal side effects. For patients who do not respond well to medications, we offer alternative therapies, including ketogenic diet therapy and surgical options like vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) and epilepsy surgery. Our dedicated team also focuses on neurodevelopmental assessments and interventions to support cognitive and behavioral development. We work closely with families to create a supportive environment, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our young patients

Headaches and Migraine

At Dhanwin Child Neuro Care, we specialize in the evaluation and management of pediatric headaches and migraines. Our team conducts thorough assessments to identify triggers and underlying causes, including lifestyle factors, diet, stress, and sleep patterns. We develop personalized treatment plans that may include medication, behavioral strategies, and lifestyle modifications to reduce headache frequency and severity. Additionally, we offer biofeedback therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), and relaxation techniques to help children manage their symptoms. Our goal is to improve the overall quality of life for children affected by headaches and migraines, enabling them to participate fully in school and recreational activities. We also provide education and support to families, helping them understand and manage their child's condition effectively.

Brain Tumors and Aneurysms

Dhanwin Child Neuro Care provides expert care for children with brain tumors and aneurysms. Our multidisciplinary team includes pediatric neurosurgeons, oncologists, radiologists, and neuropsychologists, ensuring comprehensive diagnostic evaluations and treatment planning. We offer state-of-the-art surgical interventions, including minimally invasive techniques and advanced imaging-guided surgeries. Post-surgery, our team provides ongoing management, including radiation therapy, chemotherapy, and rehabilitation services to optimize treatment outcomes. We emphasize family-centered care, supporting families throughout the treatment journey with compassion and expertise. Our holistic approach includes psychological support and counseling services to help children and their families cope with the challenges of these serious conditions.

Guillain-Barre Syndrome (GBS)

Our clinic specializes in the treatment of Guillain-Barré syndrome (GBS) and related conditions affecting children. We focus on early diagnosis through clinical evaluation, nerve conduction studies, and cerebrospinal fluid analysis. Our treatment approach includes supportive care, such as respiratory support and pain management, along with therapeutic interventions like intravenous immunoglobulin (IVIG) and plasmapheresis to manage symptoms and facilitate recovery. Our multidisciplinary team works together to minimize complications and promote functional improvement through physical and occupational therapy. We provide ongoing monitoring and rehabilitation to ensure the best possible outcomes for children affected by GBS, helping them regain their strength and independence.

CNS Infections

At Dhanwin Child Neuro Care, we diagnose and treat infections affecting the central nervous system (CNS) in children. Our team utilizes advanced imaging (MRI, CT) and laboratory techniques (CSF analysis, PCR testing) to identify causative agents and implement targeted therapies. We prioritize prompt intervention with appropriate antimicrobial treatments to reduce the risk of long-term neurological sequelae. Our comprehensive care includes supportive treatments such as anti-inflammatory medications and neuroprotective therapies. We also focus on rehabilitation services to address any residual neurological impairments, ensuring optimal outcomes for our patients. Education and support for families are integral parts of our approach, helping them navigate the challenges of CNS infections.

Autoimmune and Demyelinating Diseases

We specialize in the management of autoimmune and demyelinating disorders that affect children's nervous systems, such as multiple sclerosis (MS) and acute disseminated encephalomyelitis (ADEM). Our approach includes comprehensive diagnostic evaluations using advanced imaging, laboratory tests, and clinical assessments. We provide immunomodulatory therapies, such as corticosteroids, intravenous immunoglobulin (IVIG), and disease-modifying therapies, to minimize disease progression. Our multidisciplinary team offers supportive care, including physical therapy, occupational therapy, and counseling, to enhance quality of life. We collaborate closely with families to tailor treatment plans and provide ongoing support, empowering them to manage their child's condition effectively.

Movement Disorders

Dhanwin Child Neuro Care offers evaluation and treatment for a wide range of movement disorders in pediatric patients, including dystonia, ataxia, and tic disorders. Our team employs specialized assessments, including genetic testing, neuroimaging, and neurophysiological studies, to accurately diagnose these conditions. Treatment strategies are personalized and may include medication management, physical therapy, and neurorehabilitation techniques aimed at improving motor function and mobility. We also offer innovative therapies, such as deep brain stimulation (DBS) and botulinum toxin injections, for select cases. Our goal is to improve the overall well-being and functional abilities of children with movement disorders, helping them achieve their full potential. We provide comprehensive support to families, ensuring they have the resources and knowledge needed to support their child's development.

Neuromuscular Disorders

Our clinic offers diagnosis and treatment for a range of neuromuscular disorders affecting children. We utilize genetic testing, electromyography (EMG), and muscle biopsy to accurately diagnose conditions such as muscular dystrophies, neuropathies, and myopathies. Treatment plans are personalized and may include physical therapy, orthotics, medications, and specialized medical care to optimize muscle function and improve quality of life. We focus on slowing disease progression, managing symptoms, and enhancing mobility through a combination of therapeutic exercises, assistive devices, and surgical interventions if needed. Our comprehensive care also includes nutritional support and respiratory therapy to address the various aspects of neuromuscular disorders. We aim to enhance the overall well-being and independence of children affected by these conditions.
